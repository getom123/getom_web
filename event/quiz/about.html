GQuiz [Getom Quiz]

How it works:

Welcome to GQuiz! Here's how it works:

  • Time: The game starts 9am and ends 10:30am daily.
  • Winning Numbers: Each day, 5 lucky winning numbers are pre-announced. Check the list of winning numbers before starting the quiz.
  • Lucky Number: During the quiz, each player will be assigned a unique lucky number after completing their attempt.
  • Match and Win: If your lucky number matches one of the winning numbers for the day, you can claim your reward!
  • No Match: If your lucky number does not match any of the winning numbers, do not submit it.

Rewards:

  • Promo Code: Winners can receive a promo code for 25% off Getom services (data, airtime, tutorials, etc.).
  • Data Reward: Winners may also claim a data reward (500MB, 1GB, or 1.5GB) by submitting their mobile number and network provider through WhatsApp.

Additional Rules:

  • Each question lasts for 10 seconds. Be quick!
  • New questions and winning numbers are provided daily during the event period (Dec 15 - Dec 25).
  • One quiz attempt is allowed per day per user.
  • No cheating: All participants must follow the rules for a fair game.

NOTE:

User can only play once per day, so get your network ready!!.
